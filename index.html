About Word Funnel

Word Funnel is a specialist content studio founded by journalist and content strategist George Rajasekaran. Since 2007 we’ve helped more than 100 organizations — corporates, MSMEs, NGOs and educational institutions — clear communication bottlenecks with research-led writing, SEO-aware copy and narrative filmmaking.

Why organisations choose us

  • • Tailored solutions: Domain-specific writers and strategies for each client.
  • • SEO & readability: Content that ranks and converts without sacrificing voice.
  • • Cost-effective: Professional-grade work priced 20–30% below market to help smaller organisations access quality content.

We combine editorial rigor and practical publishing support — from drafts to publishing-ready formats and distribution guidance.

Our Story (Short)

Founded by a journalist with 20+ years' experience, Word Funnel began as a solopreneur venture near Chennai in 2007. Our founder has worked with organisations such as UNICEF, Freedom Fund, HelpAge India and national media outlets. Today a handpicked team of editors, writers and creatives delivers customised content for clients across India and internationally.

Services

Full-spectrum content services crafted to your goals — clarity, credibility and conversion.

01

Web Content Writing

SEO-friendly page copy and landing pages that clarify value propositions and guide visitors to action. We audit content gaps, craft headlines and microcopy, and deliver pages optimised for readability and conversions.

02

Social Media Content

Platform-tailored captions, content calendars and reusable assets designed to increase engagement. We match tone and frequency to your audience and include brief performance suggestions.

03

Blog Writing & Email

Research-backed blog posts and email sequences that attract organic traffic, nurture leads and articulate thought leadership. We deliver headlines, meta descriptions and distribution-ready drafts.

04

Google Ads & Ad Copy

High-impact short-form ad copy and search ad creatives that speak to intent and boost click-through rates. We test messaging variants and align copy with landing page goals.

05

Brochures & Flyers

Crisp, benefit-led brochure copy and one-pagers for events, proposals and stakeholders. We write with hierarchy for design and supply print-ready text.

06

Reports, Whitepapers & E-books

Structured long-form content with research, citations and clear executive summaries. Ideal for donors, boards and institutional audiences — we manage outlines, drafts and final formatting.

07

Press Releases & PR Copy

Media-ready press releases and announcement copy written to secure coverage. We craft strong lead paragraphs, clear quotes and distribution notes tailored to journalists.

08

Video & Documentary Scripts

Scriptwriting that balances narrative arc and practical production needs — shot lists, voiceover copy and pacing notes for promotional films, documentaries and YouTube content.

09

Resume & LinkedIn Profiles

Career narratives and profile copy that highlight achievements and translate experience into measurable impact. ATS-friendly resumes and attention-grabbing LinkedIn summaries.

10

Art of Writing — Training

Practical, hands-on training to improve clarity and structure for teams and individuals. Workshops cover storytelling, editing and applied writing for business communications.
